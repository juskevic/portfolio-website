Cosmopolitan

Cosmopolitan is a web app for home bartenders who want to explore the world of cocktails. Whether you are looking for a classic martini, a refreshing mojito, or a trendy spritz, Cosmopolitan has you covered. You can browse through a list of all cocktails, learn about their history and origins, and discover fun facts and trivia about them. Cosmopolitan is the ultimate guide for cocktail lovers.
